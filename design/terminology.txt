terminology

program:
  A collection of dates associated with workouts

workout:
  An ordered series of excercises

excercise:
  A description of actions to take

sets
  number of times to repeat either a count or duration of an action

reps
  the count of the action itself

duration
  the count of time to perform the action

action
  can be either single movement of the body, or a nested excercise

result
  record of actions performed in the context of a workout on a particular date

person
  context of which results are stored
